All About Me

Hello, my name is Shiya Wang, and I am a student majoring in Interaction and Visual Design. I believe that life is long, and it is impossible to remember every beautiful moment we experience. For me, photography is not about taking perfect pictures, but about recording life. Time passes quickly, and without being captured, many wonderful moments fade into vague memories. Photography helps me preserve these memories, making them vivid and alive again. Every time I look back at a photo in my album, my thoughts are drawn back to the very moment when the shutter was pressed, allowing me to relive that scene and emotion. In September 2024, I got my very first camera, the Sony A7C2, and since then I have been using it to document my life and the scenery I encounter during my journeys.
